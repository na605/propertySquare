UK Based Property

Working With Investors to Achieve Their Financial Goals


Property Square is a property investment concierge service that delivers end to end support to both international and UK investors who are looking for capital growth, rental income or a fixed monthly return on their capital. Our real estate professionals work in partnership with a team of legal experts to manage your chosen project. Together we handle the financial issues, including funding and tax, source the right project, manage any required renovations and then return the property to market to achieve the optimum yield.

Our diligent and passionate team make investing in UK property a simple and secure process and are dedicated to building you a fabulous financial future. Whether you are a significant cash investor or simply have a modest deposit to get things started, we can help you to achieve outstanding results from UK property investment.
